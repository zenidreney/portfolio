Meet Two Herbal Favorites: Aromatic & Medicinal

Basil (Ocimum basilicum) — A beloved aromatic herb known for its sweet, peppery scent and vibrant flavor. Perfect for culinary use, basil adds a fresh touch to dishes and is a staple in many traditional cuisines.

Melissa officinalis (Lemon Balm) — A soothing medicinal herb treasured for its calming effects. With its lemony aroma and gentle properties, it’s often used to ease stress, promote sleep, and support digestion.
